FAQ
1. What is Flexible Savings?
Flexible Savings supports crypto such as USDT, USDC, and BTC, providing stable annualized returns in line with market conditions. Users can apply for subscribe immediately and redeem at any time.
2. How are my yields generated?

For Flexible Term Products:

Your daily yield depends on the type and quantity of assets invested. It is automatically calculated and updated every hour based on the token type and market conditions. The APR may change accordingly.

Yield starts accumulating at the beginning of the next hour and is credited to your Funding Account daily at 1:00AM UTC. No yield is earned for the hour in which you redeem your assets.

Example:

Jackson invests 2,000 USDT on Jun 1, 2025, at 7:30AM UTC. Yield starts accumulating from 8AM UTC. The total yield is credited to her Funding Account daily at 1:00AM UTC.

If Jackson redeems 100 USDT at 11:30PM UTC, he will receive:

  • Yield up to 11:30PM and the principal of 100 USDT.
  • Additional yield from the remaining 1,900 USDT during the final hour.

If Jackson invests an additional 10,000 USDT on Jun 2, 2025, at 7:30AM UTC, the yield for the new investment starts from 8AM UTC and is credited to his Funding Account at 1:00AM UTC on Jun 3, 2025.

3. When can I redeem tokens I've invested in?

For Flexible Term Products: You can redeem your invested assets at any time, and they will be immediately credited to your Funding Account. To redeem, go to your Earn Account.

Please note that if the asset pool is fully utilized, the redeemable amount may be slightly lower than your invested amount.

4. What is the effective investing amount?

The Effective Investing Amount refers to the assets currently contributing to yield calculation. Newly invested assets become effective starting the next hour. For example, if you invest 66,000 USDT at 10AM UTC on Jun 1, 2025, your Effective Investing Amount will be updated at 11AM UTC to ensure accurate yield calculations.

5. What is the Tier APRs?

Savings APR tiers offer different interest rates based on the amount you stake under the Flexible Term. Rates vary by tier and may change with market conditions. To view detailed APRs, hover over the tier box on desktop or tap the info icon in the App.

For Flexible Savings, yields may include platform rewards plus a basic APR. The basic APR is floating and calculated hourly.

6. Are there any investment amount limits?

Yes. The minimum and maximum investment amounts vary by asset. You can view the specific limits when placing an order. Please note that your investment amount cannot exceed the maximum limit or the remaining pool size, whichever is lower.

7. Am I guaranteed to receive my yield even if the market price of the crypto asset drops?

For Flexible Term products, your yield is determined by the APR, which may fluctuate based on market conditions. The displayed APR is for reference only and is not guaranteed.

The product is principal-protected in token amount, meaning you'll receive at least the same number of tokens you invested. However, the actual fiat value of those tokens may fluctuate depending on market prices.

8. How is the yield distributed?

Flexible Term:

Yes. The redeemed principal and any yield accrued up to the time of redemption will be distributed immediately.

Please note that if the remaining investment amount falls below the minimum limit after partial redemption, the remaining assets will no longer generate yield.

9. Are there any other charges?

No. At present, the platform waives subscription fees and redemption fees, and does not charge management fees either.

10. During the holding period, will I be notified if the annualized yield rate of the current product is adjusted?

At present, users will not receive adjustment notifications. The annualized rate of return varies with market conditions.
